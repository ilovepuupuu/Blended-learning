Blended Learning Strategies

Matched to your digital readiness level
Level 0 · No Tech
Traditional Enriched
  • Printed activity sheets
  • Cooperative learning groups
  • Classroom debates & Socratic circles
  • Concept mapping on paper
  • Hands-on manipulatives
Level 1 · Basic AV
Audio-Visual Blend
  • Radio lessons + discussion
  • Educational TV + worksheets
  • Teacher-prepared slides (printout)
  • Photo-based case studies
  • Recorded audio explanation
Level 2 · 1 Device
Shared Screen Model
  • Teacher-led demos on projector
  • Video + pause-discuss cycles
  • PhET / GeoGebra whole-class
  • Digital storytelling display
  • One-device polling (Mentimeter)
Level 3 · Lab Access
Rotation Model
  • Lab rotation (groups take turns)
  • e-Thaksalawa LMS lessons
  • Flipped classroom (watch at home)
  • Formative quizzes (Google Forms)
  • Collaborative docs / presentations
Level 4 · 1:1 Devices
Flex / Full Blend
  • Self-paced LMS pathways
  • Project-based STEM challenges
  • Peer review & digital portfolios
  • AI-assisted feedback loops
  • Olympiad prep via e-Thaksalawa
